1998 saw many sequels and prequels in video games, such as The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time, Metal Gear Solid, Resident Evil 2, Crash Bandicoot: Warped, F-Zero X, Fallout 2, Gex: Enter the Gecko, Marvel vs. Capcom: Clash of Super Heroes, Panzer Dragoon Saga, Return to Krondor, Sonic Adventure, Soulcalibur, Street Fighter Alpha 3, and Tomb Raider III, along with new titles such as Banjo-Kazooie, Dance Dance Revolution, Half-Life, MediEvil, Parasite Eve, Radiant Silvergun, Spyro the Dragon, StarCraft and Xenogears. In Japan, Sega released the Dreamcast which was the first console of the sixth generation and also the company's last major console.
