Aidyn Chronicles: The First Mage is a 2001 role-playing video game developed by Canadian studio H2O Entertainment and published by THQ for the Nintendo 64 video game console. It was released in North America on March 20, 2001, and in Europe on August 3, 2001. The player controls Alaron, a young squire who has been poisoned and seeks a cure. The game takes place in a 3D environment and features a party system, turn-based combat, abilities, items, and skill levels.
