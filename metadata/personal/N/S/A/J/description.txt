Aero Fighters Assault is a 1997 combat flight simulation video game developed by Paradigm Entertainment and published by Vic Tokai for the Nintendo 64. It is the first and only game in the Aero Fighters series with 3D computer graphics, as well as the sixth and final title of the series before Video System filed for bankruptcy in 2001. The game pits a group of four pilots against the fictional world-dominating organization Phutta Morgana.
